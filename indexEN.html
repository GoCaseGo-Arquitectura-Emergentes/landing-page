At EquipaTour, we are committed to making every trip safe and worry-free for your clients. Our luggage management platform uses innovative technology to ensure that luggage is always under control, from the moment it is loaded to its final destination. With a variety of subscription plans tailored to your agency's needs, we are here to offer you a comprehensive solution that enhances your clients' travel experience and optimizes your operation's efficiency.

How It Works

Our luggage management platform is easy to use and highly effective. Using smart scales and impact sensors, we monitor the weight and safety of luggage in real-time. When necessary, our platform allows for the reorganization of the vehicle's load to comply with established weight limits and ensure a safe trip. All of this is done transparently and efficiently, so you can focus on providing the best possible experience for your clients.

Benefits

  • Improves luggage safety throughout the journey.
  • Optimizes luggage management, avoiding overloads and complying with established weight limits.
  • Provides peace of mind to your clients by ensuring the safety of their belongings.
  • Increases operational efficiency by eliminating luggage-related issues.
